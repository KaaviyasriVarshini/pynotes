```python
sentence = "Python is powerful"

words = sentence.split(" ")
print(words)  # ['Python', 'is', 'powerful']

joined = "-".join(words)
print(joined)  # Python-is-powerful
```

    ['Python', 'is', 'powerful']
    Python-is-powerful
